Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

It is a dish almost everyone is familiar with. It calls for simple ingredients, with the opportunity to experiment with many different combinations!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously buttter one side of a slice of bread, Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and Continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
